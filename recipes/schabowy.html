Schabowy

The best Schabowy

A polish dish similar to the Wiener Schnitzl, but obviusly much better.

Ingredients

  • 500g pork loin without bones
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp milk
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • frying oil
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Pound the meat flat, sprinkle with salt and pepper
  2. Beat the egg with milk
  3. Dip each piece in flour, egg and finally breadcrumbs, then fry them in hot oil until golden brown.
